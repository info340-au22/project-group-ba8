Events Map

Problem Overview

Joining events could be an exciting thing to many people. We are social animals and we need various events to meet friends or intake some joyful air. However, planning events can be problematic. You and your friends decided to have dinner at a nice restaurant, but there are too many in town. You planned to go to a concert that you have want to go for a long time , but you have an appointment with your doctor ten mintues after the last song ends. Guess who cannot still until the final song. You occasionally got a discounted weekend trip to Hawaii, but online travel guidelines are long and boring to read.

We had too much headache when managing events neatly and clearly. There are plenty solutions on the market, but non of them provides an integrated experience from the start to the end of an event. We have Microsoft ToDo, a todo list sharable with friends, but it's good only for brief tasks instead of comprehensive events. We have various social apps, where we can share plans and links with each other, but we need to jump back and forth between apps if you have multiple links and there are lots of useless information. We also have Tripadvisor which has a robust ecosystem for travel management, but it falls short if you made your own events. These platforms also have strong risks of fake content that deceives users, said by Forbes. Moreover, one can cost a lot of time traversing through different platforms to collect and write down information of an event, or a poorly informed event could cost time of the attendees. Integrating the experience into one platform could be a life-saver.

App Description

So we want to offer our users a web app that can create event maps! Users can also check what their friends are doing by looking at their event maps.

Designated Functions

  • Import multiple maps or create one. Maps can be public and edited by anyone
  • Mark, edit, and add descriptions of places on maps
  • Freely add public or private events and mark them on maps, create personalized event cards
  • Create differnt forms of schedule for one's events
  • Set up alarm clocks to remind users of their events
  • Check on other users' schedule and events.
  • Search through or filter events
  • Share photos and other details using the event cards
